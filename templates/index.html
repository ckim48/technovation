About

Fostering a supportive environment for mental health and wellbeing.

The Wellbeing Consultation Platform (WCP) is an innovative web application that prioritizes connecting people within high school communities, fostering a supportive environment for mental health and wellbeing. Utilizing advanced database queries and AI technology, WCP facilitates the formation of communities by strategically matching individuals with similar issues and concerns.

App Functions and Features

Personalized Wellbeing Assessment

Utilize AI chatbots to conduct personalized assessments of students' mental health and wellbeing, providing tailored recommendations for support via web interface.

Data Analytics Dashboard

Provide educators and administrators with a web-friendly data analytics dashboard to track key metrics related to student wellbeing, including usage patterns, common issues, and intervention effectiveness.

Secure and Confidential Platform

Implement robust security measures to ensure the confidentiality and privacy of student data, fostering trust and confidence among users accessing the app via web devices.
